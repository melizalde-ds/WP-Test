About

Rock 'n' Cuddle is a store for all of your pet rock friends. We have a wide variety of rocks, minerals, gems, and crystals for you to choose from. We also have a wide variety of accesories for your pet rock. We have everything from leashes to hats to sunglasses. We also have a blog where you can learn more about your pet rock and how to take care of it. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
